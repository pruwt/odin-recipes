Ingredients

  • 500 gm pigeon peas
  • 2 cups heavy coconut milk
  • 3-4 cups light coconut milk
  • pinch of turmeric
  • green chillies
  • salt to taste
  • bit of garlic paste

    • Instructions

    1. Soak your mbaazi overnight. Drain the water in the morning and rinse with clean water. Boil the mbaazi till soft and tender
    2. Prepare all the other ingredients
    3. In your cooking pot, add the mbaazi followed by the coconut milk. Now add all the other other ingredients except the coconut cream
    4. Mix everything together and bring to boil on medium heat until the coconut milk is almost dried up. Careful not to burn your mbaazi. Turn off your heat and set aside.
    5. Take another pan and heat it up. Add in your one cup of coconut cream and bring to a boil. Keep stirring until you get a thick consistency on medium low heat.
    6. Take your mbaazi and lay them nicely in your serving bowl or dish. Drizzle the coconut cream on top of the pigeon peas - be generous ðŸ˜€. You can have it for breakfast with mahamri and some ginger tea or a coffee
